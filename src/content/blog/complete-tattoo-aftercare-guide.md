# Complete Tattoo Aftercare Guide: First 30 Days

Getting a tattoo is just the beginning. Proper aftercare is crucial for ensuring your tattoo heals correctly and maintains its vibrant appearance for years to come.

## Day 1-3: The Critical Period

### Immediate Care (First 24 Hours)

1. **Keep the bandage on** for 2-4 hours after your session
2. **Wash your hands** thoroughly before touching your tattoo
3. **Gently clean** with lukewarm water and fragrance-free soap
4. **Pat dry** with a clean paper towel (avoid regular towels)
5. **Apply a thin layer** of recommended ointment

### What to Expect

- Redness and swelling around the tattoo
- Some plasma or ink leakage (completely normal)
- Slight tenderness to touch

## Day 4-14: The Healing Phase

### Daily Routine

- **Wash 2-3 times daily** with gentle, fragrance-free soap
- **Moisturize regularly** with unscented lotion
- **Avoid scratching** even when it starts to itch
- **Don't pick** at scabs or peeling skin

### What to Avoid

❌ Swimming pools, hot tubs, or ocean water  
❌ Direct sunlight and tanning beds  
❌ Tight clothing that rubs the tattoo  
❌ Gym workouts that cause excessive sweating  
❌ Alcohol-based products

## Day 15-30: Final Healing

Your tattoo should be mostly healed by now, but the deeper layers of skin are still recovering.

### Maintenance

- Continue moisturizing daily
- Start using SPF 50+ sunscreen when going outside
- The tattoo may appear slightly dull (this is normal)
- Colors will settle and brighten over the next few weeks

## Red Flags: When to Contact Your Artist

Seek immediate attention if you notice:

- Excessive redness spreading beyond the tattoo
- Green or yellow discharge
- Fever or chills
- Severe pain that worsens over time
- Red streaks extending from the tattoo

## Long-Term Care

To keep your tattoo looking fresh:

1. **Always use sunscreen** (SPF 50+) on your tattoo
2. **Moisturize regularly** to prevent fading
3. **Stay hydrated** for healthy skin
4. **Avoid rapid weight changes** that can distort the design

## Climate Considerations for Bogotá

Living in Bogotá's climate requires special attention:

- **High altitude** means stronger UV rays - use extra sun protection
- **Humidity fluctuations** - adjust moisturizing routine accordingly
- **Cool temperatures** - keep tattoo covered in cold weather

## Recommended Products

- **Soap**: Cetaphil, Dove Sensitive, or any fragrance-free option
- **Moisturizer**: Aquaphor, Lubriderm, or Aveeno (unscented)
- **Sunscreen**: Any broad-spectrum SPF 50+ (mineral-based preferred)
